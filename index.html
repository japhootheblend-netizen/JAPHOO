Care & Info

T-Shirt Care

  • Wash inside out with similar colours
  • Cold / lukewarm water (under 30°C)
  • Mild detergent only — no bleach
  • Dry in shade, out of direct sunlight
  • Low / medium-heat iron
  • Don't stretch the fabric
  • Store folded, not hung

Cap Care

  • Hand wash only — no machine wash
  • Mild detergent in warm (not hot) water
  • Soak 15–20 mins, gently brush dirty spots
  • Rinse thoroughly
  • Air dry in shape to avoid deforming

Hoodie Care

  • Wash inside out with similar colours
  • Gentle cycle at 30°C, mild liquid detergent
  • No bleach; spin gently
  • Air dry only — shake & reshape before hanging
  • Do not iron

Exchange & Returns

  • Exchange within 10 days (non-sale items only)
  • Items must be unworn, tagged & in original packaging
  • Store credit issued — no cash refunds
